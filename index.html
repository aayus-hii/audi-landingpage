Audi R8

The safety and comfort of our customers and long-term service life of our vehicles remain our top priorities at Audi. To ensure the same we conduct a programme to constantly monitor the quality of our products.

In order to give our customers, the best of Audi experience, Audi Authorized Dealers will get in touch with 18 customers of Audi R8 across India and schedule an appointment at their workshop for performing the necessary breather pipe replacement.

The activity is specific to 18 cars of Audi R8, which were manufactured from 2016 up to and including 2018.

The breather pipe replacement process would take approximately one hour and will, of course, be performed free of charge to customer.

Climate change mitigation, the responsible use of resources, societal advancement and social justice are among the great challenges of our time. Our Path

Audi has a clear vision: to reduce CO₂ emissions and become carbon-neutral in balance as a company by 2050. To get there, we’re letting go of the things holding us back and re imagining how we bring our vehicles to the road – from the supply chain, to the emissions at our production plants, to the design of our models’ entire life cycle.
